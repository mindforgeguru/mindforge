# MIND FORGE

## AI Assisted Learning

A new way to teach, learn, and stay connected.

---

## The way things are today

- Teachers spend hours **hand-grading** every test
- Students chase classmates for **what's been assigned**
- Parents only learn about issues at the **next PTM**
- Notices live in **WhatsApp groups** that get muted
- Reports, fees, and timetables travel on **paper**

> Our school deserves better tools.

---

## What is MIND FORGE?

> A single mobile app that connects **students**, **teachers**, **parents**, and **admins** — with **AI-powered tests**, real-time grades, attendance, fees, homework, and timetables in one place.

One login. One source of truth. One simple app.

---

## Four roles, one app

| | Who | What they do |
|---|---|---|
| **Teacher** | Subject teachers | Generate tests from PDFs, mark attendance, grade, broadcast |
| **Student** | Grades 8–10 | Attempt tests, see grades, check timetable, track homework |
| **Parent** | Linked to child | Monitor child's progress, fees, attendance — daily |
| **Admin** | Office staff | Manage users, fees, academic year, timetable |

---

## What's already inside

- **AI test generator** — upload a chapter PDF, get a ready-to-publish test in under 2 minutes
- **Attendance** marked per period, visible to parents the same minute
- **Grades & report charts** with class averages
- **Timetable** that adapts to holidays
- **Fees** with structures, payments, and digital receipts
- **Homework** tracking — written and online tests
- **Broadcasts** that reach every relevant phone instantly

---

# What's new today

## A reward system that motivates students

---

## Introducing **XP & Levels**

Every productive action a student takes earns **XP** (experience points).

XP unlocks **levels**, which unlock **rewards**.

Students see their progress live. Parents see what their child has been doing. Teachers do nothing extra — XP is awarded automatically.

---

## How students earn XP

| What you do | XP you earn |
|---|---|
| Marked **present** for a period | **+5** |
| Homework submitted **on time** | **+20** |
| Homework submitted **late** | +10 |
| Test score **50–69%** | **+30** |
| Test score **70–89%** | **+50** |
| Test score **90–99%** | **+80** |
| **Perfect** test (100%) | **+120** |

> Show up. Finish your work. Ace your tests. The numbers add up fast.

---

## The level ladder — 50 levels in 10 tiers

| Tier | Levels | Title |
|---|---|---|
| Beginner | 1–5 | **Novice** I → V |
| Learner | 6–10 | **Apprentice** I → V |
| Studious | 11–15 | **Scholar** I → V |
| Skilled | 16–20 | **Adept** I → V |
| Confident | 21–25 | **Expert** I → V |
| Practiced | 26–30 | **Master** I → V |
| Wise | 31–35 | **Grandmaster** I → V |
| Inspired | 36–40 | **Sage** I → V |

| Endgame | 46–50 | **Mythic** I → V |

---

When you level up, the app shows a **confetti dialog** with your new title and total XP.

It's instant. The whole class on the leaderboard sees you climb.

# For **Students**

---

<!-- _class: role-student -->

## Your daily routine

1. **Open the app each morning.** See today's timetable on the home screen.
2. **Show up.** Every period your teacher marks you present, you earn 5 XP.
3. **Check homework** under the Homework tab. Finish before the due date for max XP.
4. **Take tests** when they arrive. Score well to climb the leaderboard.
5. **Watch your XP grow** on the home dashboard's progress bar.
6. **Unlock themes** as you level up. Make the app yours.

---

<!-- _class: role-student -->

## What it looks like

- **Home dashboard**: your XP card sits above today's timetable. Tap it for the full XP screen.
- **XP screen**: total XP, level, progress bar, recent activity, leaderboard, theme picker.
- **Tests**: one attempt only. Don't close the app or switch screens during a test — it auto-submits.
- **Notifications** for: attendance marked, new test, new grade, broadcast.

<!-- _class: role-teacher -->

# For **Teachers**

---

<!-- _class: role-teacher -->

## What changes for you

> **Nothing changes** in your daily workflow. Everything you already do — attendance, grades, homework — automatically awards XP to students.

- Mark attendance → present students get +5 XP each
- Grade a test → student gets 30/50/80/120 XP based on percentage
- Mark homework complete → student gets +20 (on time) or +10 (late)

You don't have to think about XP. **The system does it.**

---

<!-- _class: role-teacher -->

## What you can do extra (optional)

- **See any student's level** when reviewing their record
- **Check the school leaderboard** to spot rising performers
- **Encourage** the students close to a level-up

Admins can also **manually adjust XP** with a written reason — for participation, special projects, or to correct an error.

# For **Parents**

---

<!-- _class: role-parent -->

## What you'll see

- Your child's **attendance** every period, every day
- Every **test result** the moment it's graded
- Every **homework** assignment and whether your child completed it
- **Fee** balance and payment history
- **Broadcasts** from teachers — class-wide and school-wide

The app fits in your pocket. The school fits in the app.

---

<!-- _class: role-parent -->

## XP — what it means for you

XP is a **motivational tool** for your child. It rewards:

- **Showing up regularly** (attendance)
- **Doing homework** (especially on time)
- **Studying for tests** (higher scores = more XP)

The level your child has reached is a quick health check on their habits. A child stuck at a low level despite being in school for months → likely missing tests or skipping homework. Worth a conversation.

---

## Privacy and safety

- **MPIN-based login** — six-digit PIN, easy for kids and parents
- **Account approval** — only the admin can create or activate accounts
- **Soft delete** — removed students can be restored if it was a mistake
- **Per-role access** — students see only themselves; parents see only their child
- **No external sharing** — every byte of data stays on our school's server
- **Push notifications** opt-in per device

---

## Getting started

4. **Allow notifications** when asked — you'll never miss an update
5. **Trouble logging in?** Visit the office or message us on the school WhatsApp

> Login is the same MPIN for the **app** and the **website**.
